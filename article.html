OnePlus Pad 3 Review: The Best Android Tablet For Students?

By BoredTech • June 2026 • 8 min read

Contents

  • Design
  • Display
  • Performance
  • Battery
  • Verdict

Design

The OnePlus Pad 3 continues OnePlus' tradition of clean industrial design. It feels premium and surprisingly light.

Students carrying it between classes will appreciate the slim profile.

Display

The display is bright, sharp, and excellent for note-taking, videos, and multitasking.

Performance

Powered by flagship hardware, the tablet handles everything from productivity apps to gaming.

This is one of the fastest Android tablets currently available.

Battery

Battery life easily lasts a full day of classes and study sessions.

Verdict

If you're looking for an Android tablet for studying, note-taking, and media consumption, the OnePlus Pad 3 is easy to recommend.
